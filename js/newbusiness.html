Company Bio

This website is developed by a group of under-graduate students in order to present an active proposal for the problem of unorganised food sector in India. This website provides a platform which is user friendly for those businessmen/businesswomen who sell food items on the streets. Whether they are licensed or not government will provide assistance in setting up, maintaining , organising their source of income. The other option is to provide the citizen an information about all the types of food and food sellers present in their locality. Meaning a boost in business because many people can access it.
